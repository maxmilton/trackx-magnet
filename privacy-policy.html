harvest-errors Privacy Policy

This browser extension package, herein referred to as harvest-errors, is designed to collect data as per the design of the trackx client https://github.com/maxmilton/trackx/tree/master/packages/client and API https://github.com/maxmilton/trackx/tree/master/packages/app-api. Therefore it may collect data including information such as:

  • Your browsers user-agent string
  • The URL href of the pages your visit
  • Stack traces of errors on those pages
  • Any other data contained within those errors

We will never share the data. It is only imported into the TrackX dashboard https://dash.trackx.app and used as samples for TrackX development.

If you have any questions or require some assistance, please contact support@happycode.ltd.
